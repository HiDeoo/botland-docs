# Acceleration Missiles

Consecutively firing these missiles lowers their cooldown. Performing other actions resets their cooldown.

| Tier | Range | Damage | Initial cooldown | Acceleration rate | Minimum cooldown |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 3 | 200 | 2.0 | 0.3 | 0.6 |
| 2 | 3 | 250 | 2.0 | 0.3 | 0.6 |
| 3 | 4 | 300 | 2.0 | 0.3 | 0.6 |

# Stunning lasers

Allow you to fire lasers that stun the target.

Stunned targets will have an extra turn of cooldown applied as long as they weren't already stunned. You cannot stun-lock a target.

| Tier | Range | Damage |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 3 | 100 |
| 2 | 4 | 150 |
| 3 | 5 | 200 |
